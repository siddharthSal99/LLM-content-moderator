Title: The guy that messed with a drug lord 
Post Text: (sorry if anything sounds weird, original is from Brazil)

This guy is chilling at home, when a big party starts at the neighboring mansion. It is a sweet sixteen party, with lots of guests and very loud music. At 1AM the guy is fed up and calls the cops to end with the nuisance. What he didn't know is that the mansion belonged to a local, cruel drug lord.

In the morning, the complaining guy is kidnapped by the drug lord's thugs, and taken to a shack in the middle of nowhere and thrown in a small windowless room. Outside the room is Big John, a hulk of a guy, 6'8", wide as a door, huge muscles all around. The thugs talk to Big John:

\- Hey, this guy complained to the cops that the boss' party was too loud! Fuck him in the ass until he screams as loud as the party!

\- Yeah, I'll handle it - replies Big John

Our complainer is shaking with fear, huddled in the corner of the windowless room. Some time later, other thugs appear in the shack dragging another tied up man:

\- Hey, Big John! This one got caught stealing jewelry from the boss' house at the party! Cut his right hand off to teach him a lesson!

\- I'll get to it - replies Big John, now cutting his nails with a machete.

The two men are still shaking in fear when more thugs come in with another tied-up man:

 \-Big John! This guy here spent the party eyeing the boss daughter's ass! Rip his left eye off so he doesn't do that again!

\- Yeah, yeah, throw him with the others - says Big John

A few minutes later, our complainer manages to get up, and talks with Big John:

\- Hello Mr. Big John, sir. I know you are a very busy man, and I just wanted to give a little hand here, and just remind you: I'm the guy that's getting his ass fucked, OK?
